How can we help you?

GIFT RECEIVING

Do you want to send items to Kerala from your country?
We can receive your parcels, take care of customs, wrap them beautifully and deliver safely

GIFT PURCHASING

Do you want to purchase items outside Giftorea?
We can buy items from any stores in Kerala (Online/Offline) and arrange a delivery for you.

GIFT WRAPPING

Looking for gift wrapping services for your gifts?
We provide customized gift wrapping service with your preferred colors with style for all types of items.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

Do you want to ship items from Korea to your country?
We can purchase items from Korea and send them to your destination via international shipping service.
